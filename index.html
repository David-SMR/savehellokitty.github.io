THE STORY

In a distant land, where magic and adventure intertwine, a princess needs help.

The game starts in the peaceful kingdom of Alicante where Princess Hello Kitty lives. The player explores the castle and learns the basic controls. Suddenly, the villain appears and kidnaps the princess.

Five Epic Missions

Each mission presents new challenges, enemies, and unique gameplay mechanics.

The Castle Kidnapping

The game starts in the peaceful kingdom of Alicante where Princess Hello Kitty lives. The player explores the castle and learns the basic controls. Suddenly, the villain appears and kidnaps the princess.

Tutorial Exploration Cutscene
2

The Enchanted Forest

The player controls the prince as he travels through a magical forest. The forest is full of enemies and traps. The player must jump over obstacles, defeat enemies, and find a key to continue.

Combat Platforming Collectibles
3

The Dangerous Bridge

The prince reaches a long bridge above a deep canyon. The player must jump between moving platforms and avoid traps. Power-ups help the prince survive and reach the other side safely.

Moving Platforms Power-ups Precision
4

The Dark Castle

The prince enters the villain's castle. Inside, there are dark hallways, guards, and puzzles. The player must explore carefully, avoid traps, and find the tower where Princess Hello Kitty is locked away.

Puzzles Stealth Exploration
5

Final Battle and Rescue

In the final mission, the prince faces the villain in an intense battle. The player must dodge attacks and use all learned skills. After defeating the villain, the prince rescues Princess Hello Kitty and peace returns to the kingdom.

Boss Fight Skills Epic Finale
